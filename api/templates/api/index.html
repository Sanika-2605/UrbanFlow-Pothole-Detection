Frequently Asked Questions

What is UrbanFlow?

UrbanFlow is a smart platform that lets citizens report potholes instantly with photos and live locations. The system notifies responsible authorities for quicker action.

How Does It Work?

Citizens report potholes by uploading images and marking the location. MNCs and Corporators get structured reports on their dashboards to verify, manage, and resolve complaints efficiently.

Why Choose UrbanFlow?

  • Instant pothole reporting using mobile or desktop
  • AI-assisted detection and categorization of road issues
  • Custom dashboards for MNCs, Corporators, and other authorities
  • Live maps with pothole heatmaps and tracking
  • Improved civic transparency and quicker action cycle
